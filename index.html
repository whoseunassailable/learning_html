Dolphins

1. What Are Dolphins?

Dolphins are marine mammals that belong to the cetacean group, which also includes whales and porpoises.

2. Species Count:

They are known for their intelligence, playfulness, and strong social bonds.

3. Where Do They Live?

Most dolphins live in saltwater oceans, but some species, like the Amazon river dolphin, thrive in freshwater rivers.

4. Intelligence and Behavior:

    Dolphins have large brains and show complex behaviors such as problem-solving, tool use, and even self-recognition in mirrors.

    They communicate using clicks, whistles, and body language.
